Consumer Price Index Data from 1913 to 2015

Consumer Price Index (CPI-U) data is provided by the U.S. Department of Labor Bureau of Labor Statistic. This monthly pipelined data is the gas powering the always-current Inflation Calculator. The following CPI data was last updated by the government on November 17, 2015 and covers up through October 2015. The next inflation update for November has a scheduled release date of December 15, 2015.

What is the CPI? Before jumping into the data, if you want to learn about the Consumer Price Index and how it is used to calculate inflation, read this CPI article.
